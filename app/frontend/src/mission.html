Why Local Link Exists

Travel should feel closer to the city, and fairer to the people who live there.

We believe travel has become too complicated. Too many packages, listings, and tourist versions of a city can pull people away from the place they actually came to see.

Local Link gives travelers a simpler option. Instead of starting with a company, they start with a trusted local who lives there.

That makes travel easier for visitors and fairer for locals. Planning gets simpler, the trip stays flexible, and more money goes to the people sharing their city.

The Road Ahead

We are building one city at a time. The goal is simple: make local travel safer, easier, and more useful for the people who live there and the people visiting.

Phase 1

Prove the model city by city

Launch in a few cities, check locals carefully, and learn from real trips before growing further. The first goal is to prove that this simpler way to travel works.

Phase 2

Strengthen the local network

Improve matching, trust checks, reviews, and the way locals show what they offer, so each city builds a stronger and more reliable network.

Phase 3

Make the trip simpler

Bring more of the planning and support into one place without turning the trip into another fixed tour.

Phase 4

Set a new standard for travel

Make trusted local connection the normal way people enter a city, so more money goes to the people who actually know the place.
