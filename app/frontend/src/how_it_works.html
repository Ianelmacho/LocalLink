Local Link makes travel simpler, more personal, and closer to the city.

Instead of starting with a big travel company, you start with a trusted local who lives there. That makes the trip easier to plan and more personal from the beginning.

The idea is simple.

Local Link starts with people, not tour packages. Travelers say what they want, locals say how they can help, and the platform helps both sides connect with clear expectations and strong safety.

  • For travelers: less time lost in listings and fixed tours, and more help from someone who actually knows the city.
  • For locals: a direct way to use their city knowledge, hosting, planning help, and translation skills.
  • For both: clear expectations, better communication, and a safer start before the trip begins.

Traveler details

Travelers share the city, their interests, their pace, and what kind of help they want.

Local details

Locals share where they live, what they can help with, when they are available, and whether they can help with translation or planning.

Good match

When both sides fit, they connect to talk through the details and the kind of trip they want to create.

Safety

Local Link is there to keep the connection safe, clear, and respectful for both the traveler and the local.

Why it feels different.

Most travel companies sell a fixed plan first. Local Link starts with a real local person, so the trip can fit you instead of pushing you into the same version of the city as everyone else.

Start with your trip

Instead of browsing ready-made packages, you start with what matters to you.

Keep it flexible

A local can guide, host, help with planning, and in some cases act as a translator depending on what they offer.

More money stays local

The people who actually live in the city stay at the center of the trip and the money from it.

How each side uses it.

The same system works for both sides, but each side uses it a little differently.

Joining as a Traveler

  • Start with the city, timing, interests, and type of support you want.
  • Ask for neighborhoods, food, planning help, logistics, or translation if that matters to the trip.
  • Connect with a local whose offer, pace, and style fit what you are trying to create.

Joining as a Local

  • Share your city, your availability, and what you can genuinely help with.
  • Offer guiding, planning help, language help, or local support on your own terms.
  • Connect with travelers whose needs match your strengths and boundaries.

Why start with a pilot.

The pilot is our first small launch. It lets us test the service in real cities, improve safety, and grow carefully.
